About Us

A church serving the Chinese community in Auckland

Light of Life, Bread of Life Christian Church seeks to be a Christ-centred, Bible-grounded spiritual family connected by love.

We value worship, truth, prayer, discipleship, and care for the community, welcoming people of different ages and backgrounds to be received, built up, and sent out.
